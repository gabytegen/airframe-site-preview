FAQ
What is Airframe?
Airframe is a transformation intelligence platform for technology leaders. It continuously monitors 17,000+ AI tools, maps real adoption data from 140,000+ company stacks, and structures every technology decision — so CIOs, CTOs, and VPs of Engineering can move with confidence in a market that moves faster than any team can track.
Who is Airframe built for?
Airframe is built for leaders at tech companies who own a budget and feel the pressure of keeping up with a market that moves faster than any spreadsheet can track. That includes functional leaders across finance, operations, marketing, and RevOps, as well as founders and CEOs managing their full technology footprint.

How is Airframe different from traditional analyst research?
Traditional analyst reports are published once or twice a year and funded in part by the vendors they evaluate. Airframe is continuously updated, vendor-neutral, and built on real adoption signals from 140,000+ company stacks — not surveys, vendor submissions, or sponsored research.
Is Airframe vendor-neutral?
Yes. Airframe has no commercial relationships with the tools it tracks. Every signal — adoption data, peer benchmarks, and use case coverage — comes from verified company stack data, not advertising or vendor funding.
What is transformation intelligence?
Transformation intelligence is the continuous synthesis of market data, peer adoption signals, and organizational context into structured decisions. It is the category Airframe defined — distinct from static analyst reports, point-in-time consulting, or vendor-funded research.
What is Airframe Discover?
Discover is Airframe's market intelligence product. It shows what peer companies actually run — filtered by size, industry, and growth stage — across 140,000+ real company stacks. Every evaluation starts from real adoption data, not vendor claims.
What is Airframe Folio?
Folio is your organization's AI stack grader. Upload your current tools and get a grade across AI readiness, redundancy, and cost exposure, with a roadmap for what to keep, cut, or replace. Folio also monitors your stack continuously, alerting you when a pricing model changes, a renewal approaches, or a better alternative emerges.
What is Airframe Studio?
Studio is a structured brief builder for software evaluations. Describe what you're solving for, add the tools you're considering, and Airframe generates a full recommendation with tool-by-tool analysis, risk factors, and next steps. Every decision gets stored automatically in Folio as a permanent record for your team.
How is Airframe different from a vendor management tool?
Vendor management tools track contracts. Airframe grades decisions. It connects what you have today to what AI has made possible, and tells you what to do about it before the next budget cycle forces the conversation.
What AI tools does Airframe track?
Airframe indexes 17,000+ AI and software tools across infrastructure, developer tools, security, productivity, data platforms, and vertical-specific applications. The index is updated continuously as new tools emerge and market positions shift.
Is Airframe a software review platform?
No. Review platforms surface individual user ratings. Airframe surfaces organizational adoption intelligence — what companies like yours actually run, not what individual users rate. It is built for technology leaders making organizational decisions, not individual software evaluation.
